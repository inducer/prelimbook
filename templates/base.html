Welcome

The DAM prelim book lets you tap into the wisdom of previous generations of grad students, and now without having to root through piles of yellowing paper that's been ripped apart by the student before you.

Please help get the electronic prelim book off the ground. Jean still has the paper version, which is now split into a "scanned" and a "non-scanned" part. If everybody chips in and scans a few pages, we'll be there in no time.

If you have not submitted your prelim yet, now's an excellent time to do so. Just click "Contribute" on the right.
